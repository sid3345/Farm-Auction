FAQs

How to Change Account Information?

  • Go to Account
  • Select the field to be edited
  • Make necesarry changes
  • Click on "Save Changes" to update account information

How to Make Payment to Add Deposit?

  • Click on Make Payment
  • Select your Payment Option
  • Enter the Account Number and Amount to be added
  • Click on Submit

How to Add a Crop For Bidding?

  • Go to Add Crop
  • Fill the Crop Details completely and upload few pictures of your crop
  • If you want to sell multiple crops, please post a different bid for each crop
